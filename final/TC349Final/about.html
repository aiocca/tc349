About Alec

Hello, my name is Alec Iocca. I'm a Media And Information student at Michigan State University, currently in my final year. I plan on going to graduate school and pursuing a Master of Science degree in Game Design. Hoping one day to be a game designer myself, this site is dedicated to opinion articles created by me. I'll be periodically making articles with no timeframe to date. Each will highlight something that I believe is crucial to good game design in a specific genre or overall. Thanks for taking the time to read this and enjoy your visit.

About Articles

As explained in the 'About Alec' section of this page, each article will highlight things that Alec finds interesting or important to make a game really good. Along with the articles there will be a component at the end of each of them to rate how good you think the article is in a couple of categories, as well as getting an average of the four criteria you'll rate it on.
